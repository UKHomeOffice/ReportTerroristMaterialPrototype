What is a COL number

You would have received a COL number when you made the order online. The number would have been in the email we sent you as confirmation of the order. COL numbers typically look like this HMPO45678ERF0.

If you can’t provide the COL number you may have to send copies of the certificate you ordered through the post.

Help finding account number

Your account number would have been given to you when you ordered the certificate. It will have been given to you when you telephoned to make the original order. Account numbers start with HMPO and typically look like this HMPO45678ERF0

If you can’t provide the reference number you may have to send copies of the certificate you ordered through the post.

Your order number would have been given to you when you ordered the certificate. It will be on any email or postal correspondence regarding the order. Orders start with HMPO and typically look like this HMPO45678ERF0

If you can’t provide the reference number you may have to send copies of the certificate you ordered through the post.
