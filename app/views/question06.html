What is a COL number

You would have received a COL number when you made the order online. The number will be in the confirmation email you would have received from us. COL numbers typically look like this COL123456/2015

Help finding account number

Your account number would have been given to you when you ordered the certificate. It will have been given to you when you telephoned to make the original order. Account numbers start with HMPO and typically look like this HMPO45678ERF0

If you can’t provide the reference number you may have to send copies of the certificate you ordered through the post.
